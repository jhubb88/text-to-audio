How to Use

  1. Upload a .txt file using the upload area below, or type/paste text directly into the text box.
  2. Choose a voice from the dropdown (options depend on your browser/OS).
  3. Adjust speed and pitch to your preference using the sliders.
  4. Click Play to hear your text read aloud.
  5. Click Stop at any time to halt playback.
