How to Use

  1. Upload a .txt file or paste text directly into the text box (up to 3,000 characters per request).
  2. Pick a voice — 10 neural voices across English, Spanish, French, and German.
  3. Adjust speed from 0.5× to 2×.
  4. Click Play. The text is sent to AWS Polly and the resulting mp3 plays in your browser.
  5. Click Stop to halt playback or cancel an in-flight request.
